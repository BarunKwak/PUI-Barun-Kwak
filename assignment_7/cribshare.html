Peer Economy
December 2018
Interaction Design Studio, 05-651

Role: User Interface Designer



Peer economy baby furniture exchange for new parents. In this project, my team created a responsive design to make it easy for new parents to either find baby furniture or get rid of furniture through a peer economy service, CribShare.



Final Design





Learnings

  • Learned how to examine stakeholder and user needs through research
  • Took into account context and constraints through our personas
  • Learned how to incorporate a peer economy concept to a service
  • Practiced designing high-fidelity mock-ups in Sketch and prototyping them in InVision
